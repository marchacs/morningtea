ANXIOUS

Anxious Morning Routine:

  1. Open curtains or blinds
  2. In bed meditation to calm anxiety and help focus the mind on the day
  3. Grab tea or water to drink. It is best to avoid caffeine when anxious because it only heightens the anxiety you are feeling.
  4. Play light piano music on either Spotify or Apple music there are plenty of calming playlists
  5. Make you bed- making your bed will make your room look clean and tidy in order to relax you. If you need extra motivation to make your bed every day watch this speech. He explains that if you make you bed that is one task done for the day and if you can get that done you can do everything else too. If everything else fails during the day at least you come home to a nicely made bed that is welcoming to you.
  6. Breakfast time! Try to each something warm in order to keep you feeling comfortable. You want to eat a healthy breakfast to ensure that you can complete all of todays tasks.
